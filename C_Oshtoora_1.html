  • Ras Sudr, Red Sea
    Egypt

  • Area:
  • 22250 sqm

  • Phase
  • Completed, April 2014

Oshtoora, a music festival who took place in a valley close to the coastal highway in Ras Sudr in South Sinai. Our design team was responsible for designing a temporary village using local and environmentally friendly materials which could be constructed and removed without leaving any footprint, it should also be packed and stored to be reused in their next events. The structure was mainly wood covered with Henna Sheets (provide a shading solution with air ventilation).
http://oshtoora.com/festival/overview/
